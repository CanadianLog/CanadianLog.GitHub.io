Welcome to The Neon Dark Setup by ThePieAlien.
This setup is based arround the TwoPixel - Icon Pack.
Sadly the developer of this pack does not like ports so the icons can't be shared.
The wallpaper and clock widgets are also recreations of those used in the origional theme's screenshots, despite these being made by me they will not be released as releasing could fall under copyright infringement.
The phone screens shown are:
Lockscreen, Homescreen, JB folder, Apple folder, Music widget HS page, Settings
If you have any questions please comment in the reddit post for this setup.
